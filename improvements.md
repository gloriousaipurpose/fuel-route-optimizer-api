# Future Improvements & AI Capabilities

## Future Improvements
- **Real-Time Traffic & Weather**: Adjust optimal speeds and fuel consumption rate dynamically.
- **Multi-Vehicle Profiles**: Allow selecting truck type, tank capacity, and fuel efficiency (mpg) in the UI.
- **Offline Router Fallback**: Integrate local routing engines (e.g., Valhalla or offline OSRM instance) to run completely network-free.
- **Stops Amenities Filtering**: Filter fuel stops by amenities like showers, parking availability, or dining options.

## What We Can Do With AI
- **Fuel Price Forecasting**: Train an ML model on historical price datasets to predict upcoming price surges and adjust purchasing schedules in advance.
- **Driver Behavior Adaptation**: Learn driver preferences (e.g., preferred brands, stop frequency) from historical logs to customize routes.
- **Smart Data Sanitization**: Employ LLMs to clean and auto-correct misspelled/incorrect street addresses in raw fuel CSV uploads.
- **Conversational Route Assistant**: Integrate a chat interface to allow scheduling routes via voice or natural language queries (e.g., "Find me a route to Denver avoiding toll roads with the cheapest diesel").
